The Xampli Framework

Measuring maturity the way it actually works in organisations.

Built on academic research and validated through structured expert interviews, the Xampli framework gives organisations an honest, evidence-based picture of where they truly are - not where they think they are.

Two Levels of Assessment

Not every situation requires the same depth

Xampli assessments are designed to be proportionate. A lot can be derived from an initial scan, and depending on where the organisation is, a full diagnostic may not be necessary to start organising action.

1
Executive Readiness Scan

Rapid assessment across 8 transformation-critical dimensions. Identifies priority risks and immediate actions. Suitable before launching or scaling transformation initiatives.

  • Single-respondent view from leadership
  • 8 dimensions, scored 1–5
  • Overall Readiness Level (ORL)
  • Transformation Confidence Indicator (TCI)
  • Available as a free online scan
2
Full Diagnostic

Deep dive across all 8 dimensions with sub-level scoring, cross-functional perspectives, and benchmarks against comparable organisations.

  • Multi-respondent across functions and levels
  • Sub-dimension scoring and gap analysis
  • Benchmarks against comparable organisations
  • Executive Action Plan - prioritised 90-day roadmap
  • Stakeholder workshop and progress review
Maturity Levels

Five stages of transformation readiness

Maturity is not binary - it exists on a spectrum. Organisations rarely mature uniformly across all dimensions. Understanding where you sit honestly is the foundation of everything that follows.

1
Exploring
Digital and AI activity is fragmented, experimental, or technology-led without strategic alignment. Significant transformation risk.
High Risk
2
Emerging
Initiatives exist but lack coordination, governance clarity, or measurable value tracking. Pilots are possible but scaling is risky.
Pilots Possible
3
Structured
Transformation priorities are defined and supported by initial governance and delivery capability. Controlled scaling becomes feasible.
Controlled Scaling
4
Scaling
Programmes are coordinated, data foundations support deployment, and leadership actively steers execution. Enterprise rollout is viable.
Enterprise Viable
5
Transforming
Transformation is embedded across strategy, operations, workforce capability, and value realisation. Optimisation is the primary focus.
Optimisation Phase

Two Key Indicators

Understanding your result

The Xampli framework produces two indicators that together give a complete picture of where an organisation stands and whether it is ready to execute transformation successfully.

ORL
Overall Readiness Level
"How developed is your transformation system right now?"
The ORL reflects your organisation's current maturity position across all eight dimensions. It gives a clear, evidence-based view of where the transformation system stands today - not where leadership hopes it is, but where it actually is based on consistent organisational reality.
Based on all 8 dimensions
Strategy Alignment Leadership Ownership Data Foundations Technology Enablement Workforce Capability Execution Structure Governance Awareness Value Realisation
TCI
Transformation Confidence Indicator
"Can this organisation execute transformation without major risk of failure?"
The TCI focuses on the four execution-critical dimensions - the ones that most determine whether transformation programmes succeed or stall. An organisation can have a reasonable ORL but a low TCI, which signals that while some maturity exists, the conditions for safe scaling are not yet in place.
Based on 4 execution-critical dimensions
Strategy Alignment Data Foundations Execution Structure Governance Awareness
